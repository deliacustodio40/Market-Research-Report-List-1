<p><ul><li>Active Mixers</li><li>Passive Mixers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF mixers are essential components in various communication systems, converting signals from one frequency to another. Active mixers utilize active devices, such as transistors or amplifiers, to perform the frequency conversion process, offering high linearity and sensitivity. On the other hand, passive mixers rely on passive components, such as diodes or transformers, to mix the input signals, resulting in a simpler design and lower cost. The market for RF mixers encompasses both active and passive types, catering to diverse applications and preferences within the industry.</p></p>
