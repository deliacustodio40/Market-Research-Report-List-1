<p><p>Continuous high shear paddle mixers and batch high shear paddle mixers are two types of mixers commonly used in the market. </p><p>Continuous type mixers are designed for continuous and uninterrupted mixing of materials. They have a constant feed of materials and produce a continuous output. This type of mixer is ideal for large-scale industrial applications where a constant and consistent mixing process is required.</p><p>On the other hand, batch type mixers are designed for mixing materials in batches. They have a limited capacity and need to be emptied and refilled after each batch is processed. This type of mixer is suitable for smaller-scale operations that require flexibility in mixing different materials or producing smaller quantities.</p></p>
