<p><p>Hygrometers are instruments used to measure humidity levels in various environments. The market for hygrometers is categorized into different types based on their measurement techniques. Capacitive hygrometers rely on changes in the electrical capacitance of a material to determine humidity. Resistive hygrometers use changes in electrical resistance to measure humidity. Thermal hygrometers utilize the cooling effect of moisture to calculate humidity levels. Gravimetric hygrometers measure humidity by analyzing changes in the weight of a substance. Other types may include optical, dew point, or psychrometric hygrometers, employing different principles to assess humidity.</p></p>
