<p><p>The Thermocouple Alloys Market includes various types of alloys such as K Type, E Type, N Type, J Type, and Other Types. K Type thermocouples are composed of a nickel-chromium alloy and a nickel-aluminum/nickel-silicon alloy. E Type thermocouples consist of a nickel-chromium constantan alloy. N Type thermocouples are made of a nickel-chromium-silicon alloy and a nickel-silicon-magnesium alloy. J Type thermocouples are composed of a copper-nickel alloy and an iron-nickel alloy. Other Types include T Type, B Type, S Type, and R Type, based on different alloy compositions and temperature ranges they can handle.</p></p>
