<p><p>The Battery Information Management System (BMS) market can be categorized into two types: Centralized BMS and Distributed BMS. Centralized BMS involves a single control unit that manages and monitors all batteries within a system, providing centralized control and data analysis. On the other hand, Distributed BMS consists of multiple control units distributed across various batteries, offering decentralized control and monitoring capabilities. These different BMS types cater to diverse requirements and offer varying levels of control and management for battery systems.</p></p>
