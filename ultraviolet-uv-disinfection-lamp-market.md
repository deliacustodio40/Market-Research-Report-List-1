<p><p>An Ultraviolet (UV) Disinfection Lamp is a device that utilizes UV-C light to disinfect surfaces and air by killing or inactivating microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, and fungi. With the increasing awareness of the importance of maintaining hygiene and preventing the spread of diseases, the demand for UV disinfection lamps is expected to surge.
