<p><p>Dimming glass, also known as smart glass, is an innovative technology used in architectural and automotive industries. There are several types of dimming glass available in the market. PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) glass uses an electric current to rearrange liquid crystals, making the glass transparent or opaque. Electrochromic glass uses a layer of electrochromic material that changes its color and transparency when a small voltage is applied. Other types of dimming glass include suspended particle device glass, photochromic glass, and thermochromic glass. These technologies provide privacy, energy efficiency, and aesthetic appeal to spaces.</p></p>
