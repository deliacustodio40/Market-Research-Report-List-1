<p><p>Flexo inks are a type of ink used in flexographic printing, which is a popular method for printing on various surfaces like paper, labels, and packaging materials. There are two main types of flexo inks: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based inks contain organic solvents that help the ink dry quickly, making them suitable for high-speed printing. In contrast, water-based inks use water as a carrier instead of solvents, making them more environmentally friendly but slower to dry. Both types have their advantages and are used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
