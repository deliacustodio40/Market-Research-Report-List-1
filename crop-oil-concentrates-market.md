<p><p>Crop oil concentrates are a crucial component in agricultural practices as they enhance the performance of herbicides. The market is divided into three types based on oil concentration. Less than 15% concentrates are usually used with highly soluble herbicides and have a better rainfastness. Between 15% and 25% concentrates provide excellent wetting and spreading properties while reducing evaporation. Greater than 25% concentrates are ideal for tough-to-control weeds and provide improved herbicide absorption. The differentiation in oil concentration allows farmers to select a suitable crop oil concentrate based on specific needs and weed characteristics.</p></p>
