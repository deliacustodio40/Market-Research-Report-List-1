<p><strong>The Commercial Vinyl Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring</li><li>Homogeneous Sheet Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vinyl flooring is classified into two main types: heterogeneous sheet flooring and homogeneous sheet flooring. Heterogeneous sheet flooring consists of multiple layers, including a wear layer, printed design layer, and foam backing, which provide durability, attractiveness, and sound installation. Homogeneous sheet flooring, on the other hand, is a single layer of vinyl throughout, offering excellent durability and requires no wax or polish. Both types of flooring are widely used in commercial spaces due to their ability to withstand heavy foot traffic and provide a durable and attractive flooring solution.</p></p>
