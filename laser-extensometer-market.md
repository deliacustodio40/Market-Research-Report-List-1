<p><strong>The Laser Extensometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Built in Laser Extensometer</li><li>Frame Laser Extensometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Extensometer market consists of two main types: Built-in Laser Extensometer and Frame Laser Extensometer. The Built-in Laser Extensometer is integrated within the test specimen, offering accurate and direct measurements of elongation or strain during the test. On the other hand, the Frame Laser Extensometer is external to the test specimen, providing non-contact measurements by detecting the displacement of reflective markers attached to the specimen. Both types serve the purpose of measuring strain or deformation in materials, but they differ in terms of their integration and measurement methods.</p></p>
