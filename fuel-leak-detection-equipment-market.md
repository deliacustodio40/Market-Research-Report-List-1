<p><p>Fuel leak detection equipment is used to identify and prevent fuel leaks in various industries. There are two types of fuel leak detection equipment: positioning type and non-positioning type. </p><p>Positioning type equipment allows for precise detection and localization of fuel leaks. It uses sensors placed strategically in the fuel storage area to monitor for any leaks. This type of equipment is ideal for larger storage facilities or installations where a high level of accuracy is required.</p><p>Non-positioning type equipment, on the other hand, monitors fuel storage areas as a whole without the need for precise positioning. It detects leaks based on changes in pressure or other indicators, providing a more general detection capability. This type of equipment is suitable for smaller or simpler fuel storage systems.</p></p>
