<p><p>Keyboard synthesizers come in two main types: modular type and all-in-one type. The modular type allows musicians to customize their synthesizer by adding or removing various modules, giving them greater flexibility in sound creation. It involves separate modules for different functionalities such as oscillators, filters, and amplifiers. On the other hand, the all-in-one type integrates all the necessary components into a single unit, making it more convenient and portable. It provides ready-to-use features and settings, making it easier for beginners or musicians who prefer a simpler setup.</p></p>
