<p><p>Heterojunction (HJT) solar panels are a type of solar panel that combines different types of materials in order to improve the overall efficiency of solar energy conversion. The junction between these different layers facilitates the efficient collection of photons and reduces energy losses, leading to higher conversion efficiency.</p><p>The future outlook of the Heterojunction (HJT) solar panels market is quite promising.
