<p><strong>The Lined Check Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PFA Lined</li><li>PTFE Lined</li><li>FEP Lined</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for lined check valves can be categorized into various types based on the type of lining material used. PFA lined check valves are lined with perfluoroalkoxy polymer, which provides excellent chemical resistance. PTFE lined check valves have a lining of polytetrafluoroethylene, known for its non-stick and high-temperature resistant properties. FEP lined check valves feature a lining of fluorinated ethylene propylene, offering good flexibility and low permeability. Additionally, there are other types of lined check valves available in the market, which utilize different lining materials with specific properties to suit various applications.</p></p>
