<p><p>Electric Industrial Vacuum Cleaners are types of vacuum cleaners that are powered by electricity. They are commonly used in indoor environments where electricity is readily available. These vacuum cleaners are equipped with electric motors that generate suction power to remove dirt and debris from surfaces.</p><p>On the other hand, Pneumatic Industrial Vacuum Cleaners are powered by compressed air. These vacuum cleaners utilize compressed air to create suction and are suitable for cleaning areas with explosive or flammable materials.</p><p>Both types of industrial vacuum cleaners serve the purpose of effectively and efficiently cleaning large industrial spaces, removing dust, dirt, and other pollutants from surfaces.</p></p>
