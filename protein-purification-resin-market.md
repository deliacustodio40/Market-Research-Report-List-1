Protein A resin binds to immunoglobulin G (IgG) antibodies, Protein G resin binds to a broader range of immunoglobulins including IgG, IgA, and IgM, while Protein L resin specifically binds to immunoglobulin light chains. These resins are widely used in research, biopharmaceutical, and diagnostic applications to effectively isolate and purify target proteins.</p></p>
