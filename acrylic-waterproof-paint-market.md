<p><p>Acrylic waterproof paint is available in two main types - crystalline and water emulsion. Crystalline acrylic waterproof paint is formulated using finely ground crystals that penetrate the surface and seal it against water. This type of paint is often used for exterior applications, such as concrete walls and roofs. On the other hand, water emulsion acrylic waterproof paint is composed of water-based polymers and additives that create a protective barrier on surfaces. This type of paint is commonly used for interior applications, such as bathrooms and kitchens.</p></p>
