<p><p>The Indoor HVAC&R System market can be categorized based on various types such as refrigeration cycle, humidity control, refrigerants, and absorption chiller. Refrigeration cycle types include vapor compression, absorption, and air cycle, which determine how heat is transferred within the system. Humidity control types manage the level of moisture in indoor spaces and can be achieved through methods like dehumidification and humidification. Refrigerants types refer to the substances used to transfer heat and can be categorized as fluorocarbons, hydrocarbons, and ammonia. Absorption chillers are a type of cooling system that utilize a heat source to drive the refrigeration process.</p></p>
