<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The rubber flame retardants market includes three main types: organic halogen, organic phosphorus, and inorganic flame retardants. Organic halogen flame retardants are compounds that contain halogen atoms, such as chlorine or bromine, and are widely used for their high effectiveness. Organic phosphorus flame retardants, on the other hand, contain phosphorus and are known for their low toxicity and lower smoke production. Inorganic flame retardants, like aluminum trihydrate and antimony trioxide, do not contain carbon and are used in rubber formulations to meet flame retardancy requirements.</p></p>
