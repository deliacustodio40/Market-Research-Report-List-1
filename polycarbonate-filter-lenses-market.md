<p><ul><li>Weak Filter</li><li>Neutral Filter</li><li>Strong Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polycarbonate filter lenses are available in three market types: weak filter, neutral filter, and strong filter. Weak filter lenses have a light tint that reduces brightness and glare in mildly sunny conditions. Neutral filter lenses maintain true colors and reduce brightness in moderate to bright sunlight without altering the perception of colors. Strong filter lenses offer high protection from intense sunlight, blocking a significant amount of light and reducing glare, making them suitable for extremely bright outdoor environments. These three types provide users with options suitable for various light conditions and personal preferences.</p></p>
