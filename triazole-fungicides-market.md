Propiconazole is known for its systemic and broad-spectrum activity, making it effective against various fungal pathogens. Tebuconazole is mainly used to control diseases in cereals and grapes due to its long-lasting protective action. Difenoconazole is widely used for foliar diseases control in crops such as cereals, fruits, and vegetables, thanks to its excellent systemic action and broad-spectrum activity. These fungicides play a vital role in protecting crops and ensuring higher agricultural productivity.</p></p>
