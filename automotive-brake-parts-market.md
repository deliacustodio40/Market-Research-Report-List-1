<p><p>The automotive brake parts market consists of various types of components. Brake pads are one of the most essential parts that are pressed against the brake disc to create friction and slow down the vehicle. Brake shoes perform a similar function but are used in drum brake systems. Rotors and calipers work together to squeeze the brake pads against the brake disc, generating friction.
