<p><p>The compressed air reel market can be categorized into two main types: self-retracting and manual. Self-retracting air reels are designed with a mechanism that automatically winds up the hose after use, making it convenient and hassle-free. These reels are popular for their ease of use and safety features. On the other hand, manual air reels require the user to manually rewind the hose onto the reel after each use. They are a more cost-effective option but may require more effort and time to operate.</p></p>
