<p><p>The manual welders market includes various types, such as projection welding and spot welding. Projection welding is a process where two or more pieces of metal are joined together by applying pressure and passing an electric current through a small projection on one of the pieces. Spot welding, on the other hand, is a technique where two or more metal sheets are joined by applying pressure and passing an electric current through a small spot or point of contact between the materials. Both these methods are commonly used in industries that require manual welding operations.</p></p>
