It is widely used in various industries as an additive or coating material due to its excellent lubricating and dispersing properties. Two common types of micronized polyethylene wax powder are polyethylene wax powder and oxidized polyethylene wax powder. Polyethylene wax powder is derived from non-reactive polyethylene, while oxidized polyethylene wax powder is made by subjecting the wax to an oxidation process, resulting in improved compatibility with other materials and enhanced performance. Both types find application in a range of industries such as plastics, coatings, and personal care products.</p></p>
