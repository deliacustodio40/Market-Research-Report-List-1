<p><strong>The Helicopter Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FFS and FTD</li><li>ATD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helicopter simulator market encompasses Full Flight Simulators (FFS), Flight Training Devices (FTD), and Advanced Training Devices (ATD). FFS is a high-fidelity simulator designed to replicate the helicopter's environment, providing a realistic flight experience. FTDs offer a lower level of fidelity, mainly used for aircraft system training and procedural exercises. ATDs are cost-effective solutions, mainly used for basic flight training and familiarization. These market types cater to different training needs and budgets, ensuring that helicopter pilots receive efficient and effective training to enhance their skills and safety.</p></p>
