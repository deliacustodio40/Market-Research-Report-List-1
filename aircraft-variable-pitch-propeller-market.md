<p><p>The aircraft variable pitch propeller market is classified into several types. The first type is controllable pitch propellers, which allow pilots to adjust the propeller blade angles during flight to optimize performance. The second type is constant speed propellers, which automatically maintain a constant rotational speed regardless of aircraft speed. The third type is full feathering propellers, which can rotate the blades to a position parallel to the airflow to minimize drag in case of engine failure. Lastly, there are other types of variable pitch propellers that offer unique features and functions to cater to specific aircraft requirements.</p></p>
