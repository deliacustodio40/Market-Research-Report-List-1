<p><ul><li>Tilt-tray</li><li>Cross-belt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airports baggage sorting systems market consists of two main types: tilt-tray and cross-belt systems. Tilt-tray systems employ trays that tilt to carry bags from one point to another, allowing for efficient sorting and transfer. These systems are known for their flexibility and adaptability. On the other hand, cross-belt systems use a continuous loop belt to transport bags for sorting. They are known for their high-speed capabilities and ability to handle heavier bags. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth and accurate transfer of baggage within airports.</p></p>
