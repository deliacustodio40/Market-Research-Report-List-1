<p><p>Magnesium coatings market comprises various types, including cold coating, hot coating, thermoset, electrolysis, and others. Cold coating involves applying a protective layer on magnesium surfaces at room temperature, while hot coating employs high temperatures for the coating process. Thermoset coatings are cured by heat to create a tough, chemically resistant finish. Electrolysis utilizes an electric current to deposit protective layers on magnesium surfaces. The "others" category encompasses other coating methods such as electroplating and chemical vapor deposition. These different types cater to diverse requirements, providing corrosion resistance and improved aesthetics to magnesium products.</p></p>
