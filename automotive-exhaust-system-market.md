<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive exhaust system market consists of two main types: single exhaust system and dual exhaust system. A single exhaust system utilizes a single exhaust pipe to channel the exhaust gases from the engine to the tailpipe. It is commonly found in smaller and less powerful vehicles. On the other hand, a dual exhaust system incorporates two separate exhaust pipes that run parallel to each other. This type of system is often seen in larger and more performance-oriented vehicles, providing improved engine performance and a more aggressive aesthetic.</p></p>
