<p><p>The B-type Ultrasound Diagnostic System market encompasses various types including Fanshaped Scanning, Linear Scanning, and Mixed Type. Fanshaped Scanning involves the ultrasound probe emitting fan-shaped sound waves, allowing for a wider imaging area. Linear Scanning, on the other hand, uses a linear array of transducers to produce images with higher resolution and improved detail. Mixed Type combines the advantages of both fanshaped and linear scanning techniques, providing a more comprehensive diagnostic capacity. These different types cater to different clinical needs and contribute to the diverse landscape of the B-type Ultrasound Diagnostic System market.</p></p>
