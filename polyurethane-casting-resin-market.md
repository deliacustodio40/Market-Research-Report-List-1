<p><p>Polyurethane Casting Resin is classified into two types: Clear Resin and Opaque Resin. Clear Resin refers to a type of polyurethane casting resin that has excellent transparency and is often used in applications where visual clarity is paramount, such as jewelry making or model crafting. On the other hand, Opaque Resin is a polyurethane casting resin that lacks transparency and is typically utilized when a solid, non-transparent appearance is desired, like in prototyping or architectural models. These two types serve different purposes based on their visual properties.</p></p>
