<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The AEB (Automatic Emergency Braking) System market consists of three major types: Forward Collision Warning, Dynamic Brake Support, and Crash Imminent Braking. Forward Collision Warning alerts the driver of an upcoming collision, allowing them to take appropriate action. Dynamic Brake Support further assists the driver by providing additional braking force to mitigate the potential impact. Crash Imminent Braking, on the other hand, autonomously applies brakes if the driver fails to respond to the warnings, aiming to prevent an accident. These systems collectively aim to enhance vehicle safety by minimizing the risk of collisions.</p></p>
