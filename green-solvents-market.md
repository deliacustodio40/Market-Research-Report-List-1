Manufacturers are investing in technologies to improve the solubility, viscosity, and stability of these solvents, making them suitable for a wider range of applications.</p><p>In conclusion, the green solvents market is experiencing significant growth due to the rising adoption of environmentally-friendly alternatives and government regulations promoting sustainable practices.
