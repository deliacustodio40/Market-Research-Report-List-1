<p><strong>The Filtration Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Qualitative Filtration Papers</li><li>Quantitative Filtration Papers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filtration paper market types include qualitative filtration papers, quantitative filtration papers, and others. Qualitative filtration papers are designed for general-purpose filtration applications where relatively fast flow rates are desired and the retained particles are not required to be quantitatively analyzed. Quantitative filtration papers, on the other hand, are used when precise measurements of the retained particles are needed. These papers have controlled pore size and are commonly employed in environmental and pharmaceutical applications. The "others" market category encompasses specialized filtration papers for specific uses, such as ashless filter paper for gravimetric analysis or glass fiber filters for air pollution monitoring.</p></p>
