<p><p>Non-Thermal Pasteurization Equipment refers to various technologies used to pasteurize food and beverages without traditional heat-based methods. High Pressure Processing (HPP) relies on subjecting the product to high pressure, while Pulse Electric Field (PEF) uses electric pulses to kill microorganisms. Microwave Volumetric Heating (MVH) applies microwave energy evenly throughout the product. Irradiation involves exposing the food to ionizing radiation, while Ultrasonic uses high-frequency sound waves. Other non-thermal pasteurization methods may include enzymatic, bacteriophage, and pulsed light treatments. These technologies offer alternatives to conventional heat treatment for food preservation and safety.</p></p>
