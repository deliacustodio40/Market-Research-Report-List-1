<p><p>Modified Bitumen Waterproof Membrane is a type of roofing material that offers exceptional durability and waterproofing capabilities. There are two main types of Modified Bitumen Membranes - SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS membranes are made using synthetic rubber additives, giving them increased flexibility and resistance to cold temperatures. APP membranes, on the other hand, are modified with plastic additives, making them more heat-resistant. Both types provide excellent UV resistance and are widely used in the construction industry for various applications, including flat roofs and below-grade waterproofing.</p></p>
