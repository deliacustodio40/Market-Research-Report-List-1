<p><p>Solvent-free two-component polyurethane adhesive market types include polyether type, polyester type, and hybrid type. Polyether type adhesive is known for its good flexibility, water resistance, and low viscosity. Polyester type adhesive is recognized for its high strength, durability, and excellent heat resistance. Hybrid type adhesive combines the properties of both polyether and polyester types, offering a balance between flexibility and strength. These adhesive types cater to various applications, such as automotive, construction, packaging, and electronics, providing reliable bonding solutions without relying on solvents.</p></p>
