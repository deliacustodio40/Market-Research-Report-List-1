<p><p>The CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) Polishing Liquid market encompasses various types of slurries used in the semiconductor industry. Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurries are three common market types. Alumina Slurry utilizes aluminum oxide particles to remove material from semiconductor surfaces. Colloidal Silica Slurry consists of silica particles suspended in a liquid, providing a high removal rate for certain materials. Ceria Slurries employ cerium oxide particles, allowing for superior planarization performance, especially on copper and barrier layers. These slurries play a crucial role in achieving the desired smoothness and flatness of semiconductor surfaces during the manufacturing process.</p></p>
