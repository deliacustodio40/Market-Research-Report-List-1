<p><p>The oil and gas catalyst market consists of different types of catalysts used in the refining and processing of oil and gas. Zeolites are porous materials that help in the cracking and reforming of hydrocarbons. Precious metals such as platinum and palladium are used for catalytic reforming reactions. Rare earth metals, like cerium and lanthanum, act as catalysts in desulfurization and cracking processes. Transition metals like nickel and cobalt are utilized in hydrotreating reactions. The "Others" category includes catalysts made from non-metallic materials like silica-alumina or metal oxides.</p></p>
