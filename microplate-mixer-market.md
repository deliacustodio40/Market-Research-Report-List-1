<p><p>The microplate mixer market includes two main types: vortex mixer and orbital mixer. A vortex mixer is designed to agitate samples placed in microplates using a circular shaking motion, enhancing mixing and homogenization. It is commonly used for applications that require small volumes and quick mixing. On the other hand, an orbital mixer moves microplates in a circular motion on a flat platform, providing a gentle mixing action ideal for delicate samples or longer incubations. Both types of mixers play a crucial role in laboratories and research facilities for various applications.</p></p>
