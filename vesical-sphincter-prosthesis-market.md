<p><p>The Vesical Sphincter Prosthesis is a medical device used to treat urinary incontinence, which is the inability to control the flow of urine. It consists of an artificial sphincter that is implanted around the neck of the bladder to regulate the flow of urine. The prosthesis consists of a cuff or ring that is controlled by a balloon reservoir and a pump that is implanted in the scrotum for men or in the labia for women. By squeezing the pump, the cuff tightly closes around the neck of the bladder, preventing the unwanted leakage of urine. The prosthesis can be manually adjusted to allow emptying of the bladder at appropriate times.</p><p>The future outlook for the Vesical Sphincter Prosthesis market is promising.
