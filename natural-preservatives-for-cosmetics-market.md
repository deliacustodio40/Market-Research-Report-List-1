<p><p>Natural preservatives for cosmetics are substances derived from natural sources that help to prolong the shelf life of cosmetic products by inhibiting the growth of bacteria, fungi, and other microorganisms. Additionally, the rise in demand for natural and clean beauty products, which focus on using ingredients sourced from nature, has further fueled the demand for natural preservatives.</p><p>The natural preservatives for cosmetics market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. <p><p>The natural preservatives for cosmetics market can be classified into various types.
