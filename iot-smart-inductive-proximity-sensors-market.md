<p><p>The IoT Smart Inductive Proximity Sensors Market is categorized into various types such as IP00, IP01, IP02, IP03, IP04, and Others. IP00 represents sensors with no special resistance to dust and moisture. IP01 indicates sensors that are protected against solid objects larger than 50mm but lack resistance to moisture. IP02 sensors are resistant to solid objects larger than 12.5mm and provide protection against dripping water. IP03 sensors offer protection against spraying water, while IP04 sensors are shielded from splashing water. The "Others" category includes sensors with different specifications or protection levels not covered by the mentioned types.</p></p>
