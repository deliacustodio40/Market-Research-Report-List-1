<p><p>Thermoforming films are used in the packaging industry for various applications. Rigid thermoforming films are stiff and sturdy, typically used in the production of trays, containers, and blisters. They provide excellent protection and durability. On the other hand, flexible thermoforming films are pliable and can be easily molded into different shapes. They are commonly used for packaging delicate and irregularly shaped products like fruits, vegetables, and meat. Both types of films play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and presentation of products in the market.</p></p>
