<p><p>The automotive fuel systems market comprises three main types: gasoline systems, diesel oil systems, and compressed natural gas (CNG) or liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) systems. Gasoline systems are designed specifically to deliver and manage gasoline fuel in vehicles. Diesel oil systems, on the other hand, are responsible for storing, pressurizing, and injecting diesel fuel into the engine. CNG or LPG systems focus on alternative fuel options, utilizing compressed natural gas or liquefied petroleum gas to power vehicles, offering lower emissions and potentially reduced fuel costs.</p></p>
