<p><p>The Separated Metal Detector market consists of three main types: Electromagnetic Induction, X-ray, and Microwave detectors. Electromagnetic Induction detectors use magnetic fields to detect metal objects, making them suitable for industrial applications. X-ray detectors utilize high-energy radiation to detect and identify different types of metals, allowing for more precise inspection in industries like food processing. Microwave detectors, on the other hand, use microwave technology to identify metal objects by measuring changes in microwave signals. Each type has its own advantages and is utilized in various industries for metal detection purposes.</p></p>
