<p><ul><li>Early Strength Enhancer</li><li>Late Strength Enhancer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strength enhancers for cement are chemical additives that improve the strength and durability of cement. There are two main types: early strength enhancers and late strength enhancers. Early strength enhancers facilitate faster strength development in the early stages of cement hydration, allowing for quicker setting and early removal of formwork. Late strength enhancers, on the other hand, enhance the final strength of cement by further improving the hydration process, providing greater long-term durability. Both types are important for different construction needs and offer distinct advantages in cement applications.</p></p>
