<p><p>Self-loading concrete mixers are machines used in construction to mix and transport concrete materials. They come in two types: movable and fixed. Movable self-loading concrete mixers are designed with wheels, allowing them to be easily transported between construction sites. They offer flexibility and convenience for projects that require mobility. On the other hand, fixed self-loading concrete mixers are permanently installed at construction sites, providing a stable and continuous supply of concrete. They are suitable for projects with long-term or high-volume concrete mixing requirements.</p></p>
