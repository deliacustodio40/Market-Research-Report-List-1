<p><p>A New Energy Vehicle (NEV) Speed Reducer is a crucial component used in electric and hybrid vehicles to control the speed and torque of the electric motor. As a result, the demand for efficient and high-performance speed reducers is also growing. </p><p>Latest Trends:</p><p>1. Lightweight Speed Reducers: Manufacturers are focusing on developing lightweight speed reducers using advanced materials such as aluminum and carbon fiber composites.
