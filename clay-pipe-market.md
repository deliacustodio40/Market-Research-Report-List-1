<p><ul><li>Glazed</li><li>Unglazed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clay pipe market can be classified into two types: glazed and unglazed. Glazed clay pipes are coated with a layer of ceramic glaze, which provides them with a smooth and glossy finish. These pipes are favored for their aesthetic appeal and are often used for decorative or ornamental purposes. On the other hand, unglazed clay pipes lack the glaze layer, maintaining their natural and rustic appearance. These pipes are known for their durability and are commonly used for sewerage or drainage systems.</p></p>
