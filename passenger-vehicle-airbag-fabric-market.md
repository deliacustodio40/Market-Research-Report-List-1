<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Airbag Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Airbag Fabric</li><li>OPW Airbag Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle airbag fabric market is divided into two main types: flat airbag fabric and OPW (One Piece Woven) airbag fabric. </p><p>Flat airbag fabric is a traditional type of airbag fabric that is made by weaving individual yarns together to form a flat structure. It is widely used in passenger vehicles and provides reliable protection during collisions.</p><p>OPW airbag fabric, on the other hand, is a newer type of airbag fabric that is made by a single piece weaving technique. This innovative method eliminates the need for stitching or seams, resulting in a more durable and efficient airbag fabric. OPW airbag fabric offers enhanced safety and performance in passenger vehicles.</p></p>
