<p><p>The packaged explosive market encompasses different types such as traditional dynamite, packaged emulsions, and water gel, as well as packaged ANFO. Traditional dynamite consists of a mixture of nitroglycerin, a stabilizer, and an absorbent material. Packaged emulsions and water gel refer to explosives where the main ingredient is a mixture of oxidizers and fuels, stabilized with various agents. Packaged ANFO is a type of explosive that combines ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, commonly used in mining, construction, and quarrying industries. These different types cater to various applications within the explosive market.</p></p>
