It plays a crucial role in maintaining the desired temperature inside the cabin and providing a comfortable environment for the occupants. <p><p>The Automotive Air-condition Compressor market can be categorized into three major types. Firstly, fixed compressors are commonly used in older vehicles and have a fixed capacity to cool the air. Secondly, variable compressors adjust their capacity based on the cooling requirement, resulting in more energy-efficient operations. Lastly, electric compressors use electricity instead of a belt-driven system to power the compressor. These compressors are primarily used in hybrid and electric vehicles and offer a quieter and more efficient cooling solution.</p></p>
