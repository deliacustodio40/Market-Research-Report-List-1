<p><p>IBR Roof Market Types -Single Span and Double Span refers to the design and structure of roofing systems. Single Span roofs have a single slope or pitch and are often used for small structures like carports or storage sheds. They provide simple and cost-effective solutions. On the other hand, Double Span roofs have two slopes or pitches, creating a ridge in the middle. Double Span roofs are suitable for larger structures like warehouses or industrial buildings, offering increased strength, durability, and better drainage options.</p></p>
