<p><p>The advanced photomasks market types include quartz mask, soda mask, and others. Quartz masks are made of high-purity quartz that offers better durability, transparency, and dimensional accuracy. Soda masks are created by adding soda to the glass to improve the refractive index. Other types include materials like borosilicate and synthetic fused silica. These masks play a crucial role in the production of integrated circuits (ICs) and other electronic components. They are designed to accurately transfer intricate patterns onto silicon wafers through photolithography processes, enabling the manufacturing of advanced semiconductor devices.</p></p>
