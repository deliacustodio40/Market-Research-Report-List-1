<p><strong>The GPS Auto Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired Transmission System</li><li>Dial Transmission Mode System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS Auto Monitoring System market can be categorized into two main types: Wired Transmission System and Dial Transmission Mode System. The Wired Transmission System involves the use of cables and wires to transmit GPS data from the vehicle to a monitoring center. On the other hand, the Dial Transmission Mode System relies on dial-up connections to transmit the GPS data. Both systems facilitate the real-time monitoring of vehicle locations and provide crucial information to assist in fleet management, transportation safety, and tracking purposes.</p></p>
