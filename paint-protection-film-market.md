<p><p>The Paint Protection Film market consists of three main types: PVC Type, PU Type, and TPU Type. PVC Type paint protection films are made from polyvinyl chloride, providing good durability and flexibility. PU Type films are manufactured using polyurethane, offering excellent resistance against scratches and stains. TPU Type films, made from thermoplastic polyurethane, possess enhanced strength and elasticity. These different film types cater to various customer preferences and requirements in terms of protection, longevity, and performance against external elements, such as rocks, debris, and UV rays.</p></p>
