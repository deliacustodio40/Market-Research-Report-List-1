<p><p>The veterinary excipients market can be categorized into two types - animal-based and plant-based. Animal-based excipients are derived from animal sources such as gelatin, lactose, and collagen, and are used in various veterinary formulations. They provide functionalities like increased stability and improved drug delivery. On the other hand, plant-based excipients are derived from plants and include substances like starch, cellulose, and gums. These excipients are used in veterinary products for their binding, disintegration, and lubricating properties. Both types play a crucial role in the development and formulation of veterinary medicines.</p></p>
