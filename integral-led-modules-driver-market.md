A Constant Current Driver is designed to maintain a constant level of current flowing through the LED modules, ensuring stable and consistent performance. On the other hand, a Constant Voltage Driver provides a steady voltage output to the LED modules, allowing them to operate at a predetermined voltage level. These drivers are important components in the LED lighting industry, enabling efficient and reliable control of LED modules.</p></p>
