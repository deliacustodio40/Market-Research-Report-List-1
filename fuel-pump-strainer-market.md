<p><p>The fuel pump strainer market can be categorized into two types: OEM and aftermarket. OEM refers to Original Equipment Manufacturer, where the strainers are produced and supplied by the original vehicle manufacturer. These strainers are specifically designed to meet the exact specifications and requirements of the vehicles. On the other hand, aftermarket strainers are produced by third-party manufacturers and are available for purchase after the vehicle has been sold. These strainers may or may not match the exact specifications of the OEM strainers and are often more affordable options for vehicle owners.</p></p>
