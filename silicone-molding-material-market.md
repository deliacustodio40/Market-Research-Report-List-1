<p><p>Silicone molding material market offers two main types: press molding and injection molding. Press molding involves pressing the silicone material into a pre-designed shape using pressure and heat. It is often used for creating small to medium-sized products with simple designs. On the other hand, injection molding utilizes a mold that is filled with liquid silicone material, injected under high pressure. This method allows for producing complex and precise products with high efficiency. Both press molding and injection molding play crucial roles in the silicone molding material market, catering to different product requirements.</p></p>
