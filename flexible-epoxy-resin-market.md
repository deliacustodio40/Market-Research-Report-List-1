<p><p>The flexible epoxy resin market includes various types such as urethane modified, rubber modified, and dimer acid. Urethane modified epoxy resins are created through the addition of urethane monomers, enhancing flexibility and toughness. Rubber modified epoxy resins involve the incorporation of rubber particles, enhancing impact resistance and flexibility. Dimer acid modified epoxy resins are achieved by replacing a portion of the epoxy resin with dimer acid, offering improved flexibility, chemical resistance, and adhesion. These different types cater to diverse applications, providing a wide range of flexible and high-performance options to various industries.</p></p>
