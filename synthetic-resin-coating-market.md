<p><strong>The Synthetic Resin Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Type</li><li>Water Type</li><li>Solvent-free Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Synthetic Resin Coating market is categorized into three types based on the solvents used. Solvent Type coatings are made with solvents like toluene or xylene, which evaporate during the drying process, leaving a protective resin layer. Water Type coatings use water as a solvent instead of traditional solvents, making them more eco-friendly and low in volatile organic compounds. Solvent-free Type coatings do not contain any solvents and are cured by other methods such as heat or ultraviolet light. They offer excellent durability and environmental benefits.</p></p>
