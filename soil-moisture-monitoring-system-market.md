<p><p>Soil Moisture Monitoring System market includes various types of systems such as FullStop System, Tensiometers System, Granular Matrix Sensors System, Capacitance System, and Other System. The FullStop System measures soil moisture by using a device that directly stops water flow through the soil. Tensiometers System uses a vacuum gauge to measure the soil water potential. Granular Matrix Sensors System utilizes sensors placed in the soil to measure moisture content. Capacitance System measures moisture by analyzing changes in electrical capacitance. Other Systems involve different techniques to monitor soil moisture levels.</p></p>
