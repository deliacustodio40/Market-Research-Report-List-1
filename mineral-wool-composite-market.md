<p><p>The mineral wool composite market consists of two main types: rock wool composite and glass wool composite. Rock wool composite is made from volcanic rock or basalt, which is melted and spun into fibers. It is widely used for its excellent fire resistance and sound absorption properties. On the other hand, glass wool composite is made from recycled glass, which is also melted and spun into fibers. It is known for its thermal insulation capabilities and is commonly used in the construction industry. Both types of mineral wool composites offer distinct benefits in different applications.</p></p>
