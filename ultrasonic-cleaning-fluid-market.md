<p><p>The ultrasonic cleaning fluid market can be categorized into two types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based cleaning fluids are composed mainly of water and contain additives like surfactants and detergents to enhance their cleaning properties. They are environmentally friendly, non-toxic, and safe to use on a wide range of materials. On the other hand, solvent-based cleaning fluids contain organic solvents as their base. They are more effective in removing oils, grease, and tough contaminants, but they can be flammable and potentially harmful to the environment and human health.</p></p>
