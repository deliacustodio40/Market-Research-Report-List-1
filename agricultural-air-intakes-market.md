<p><p>Agricultural air intakes can be categorized into two types - inlets for walls and roofs and motorized intakes. Inlets for walls and roofs are designed to allow the flow of clean air into agricultural buildings while preventing the entry of dust, debris, and other contaminants. These inlets can be manually adjusted to control the amount of incoming air. On the other hand, motorized intakes are equipped with motors that automatically regulate airflow based on pre-set parameters, ensuring optimal ventilation in agricultural structures.</p></p>
