<p><p>Adhesive layers for ostomy bags consist of various types of products that help secure and protect the ostomy appliance. Stoma paste is used to create a seal between the skin and the ostomy bag. Stoma rings are used to fill uneven skin surfaces around the stoma. Barrier film offers an extra layer of protection against moisture and irritation. Adhesive remover helps dissolve and remove the adhesive from the skin. Other products may include skin prep wipes and adhesive strips.</p></p>
