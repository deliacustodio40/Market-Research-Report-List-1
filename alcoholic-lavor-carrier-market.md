<p><p>The alcoholic flavor carrier market comprises two main types: flavor emulsion and cloud emulsion. Flavor emulsion is a market segment that involves the dispersion of alcoholic flavors in water using emulsifying agents. This results in the creation of a stable emulsion with a uniform distribution of flavor particles. On the other hand, cloud emulsion is a market segment that focuses on the formation of cloud-like turbidity in alcoholic beverages using emulsion stabilizers. This gives the beverages a visually appealing cloudy appearance while maintaining the desired flavor. Both market types aim to enhance the sensory experience of alcoholic beverages.</p></p>
