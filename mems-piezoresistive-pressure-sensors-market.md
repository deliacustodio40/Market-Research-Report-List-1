Relative (Gauge) Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure. Differential Pressure Sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points. These sensors find applications in various industries including automotive, medical, and industrial sectors. Each sensor type caters to specific pressure measurement requirements, providing crucial information for safety, efficiency, and accuracy in a wide range of applications.</p></p>
