<p><p>Skin packaging films are used for protective packaging of various consumer products. These films are primarily made of different materials such as LDPE (low-density polyethylene), PVC (polyvinyl chloride), ionomer, and other materials. LDPE films provide excellent clarity and flexibility, making them suitable for packaging irregularly shaped items. PVC films are known for their exceptional strength and durability. Ionomer films offer high clarity, heat resistance, and puncture resistance. Other materials used in skin packaging films include PET (polyethylene terephthalate) and PP (polypropylene), which offer different levels of clarity, tensile strength, and temperature resistance.</p></p>
