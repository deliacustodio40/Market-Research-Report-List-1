<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Press Forging Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forging</li><li>Open Die Forging</li><li>Extrusion</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Press forging machinery refers to the equipment used in the process of press forging, which involves shaping metal by applying pressure through dies. There are various types of press forging machinery. Closed die forging involves shaping metal between two dies, resulting in a highly precise final product. Open die forging, on the other hand, involves shaping metal with dies that do not enclose the entire workpiece, resulting in a more relaxed metal structure. Extrusion involves squeezing metal through a shaped opening, while other types include upsetting, coining, and precision forging.</p></p>
