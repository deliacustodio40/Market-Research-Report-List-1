<p><p>Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) technology is a technique used to create a map of an unfamiliar environment while simultaneously determining the location of the device or robot within that environment. SLAM is an essential technology for autonomous systems, robotic navigation, augmented reality, and virtual reality applications.</p><p>The future outlook for the SLAM technology market is promising.
