<p><p>The All-Wheel Drive Motorcycles market is segmented into two types: Disc Braking System and Drum Braking System. The Disc Braking System utilizes a disc rotor and calipers to provide stopping power, offering improved efficiency, better heat dissipation, and enhanced control. On the other hand, the Drum Braking System employs shoes pressed against the inner surface of a drum to slow down the vehicle. While drum brakes are less expensive and simpler, they can generate excess heat and are generally considered less effective than disc brakes in terms of stopping power and control.</p></p>
