<p><strong>The Veterinary Cephalosporin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>First Generation</li><li>Second Generation</li><li>Third Generation</li><li>Fourth Generation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary cephalosporin market is categorized into four generations based on the evolution of these drugs. First-generation cephalosporins are effective against a limited range of bacteria, particularly Gram-positive strains. Second-generation cephalosporins broaden the spectrum of activity to cover more Gram-negative bacteria. Third-generation cephalosporins are even more effective against Gram-negative bacteria and are commonly used to treat respiratory and urinary tract infections. Fourth-generation cephalosporins have an extended spectrum of activity against both Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria, including drug-resistant strains. Each generation represents an advancement in the effectiveness and coverage of these antibiotics.</p></p>
