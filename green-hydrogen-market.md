<p><strong>The Green Hydrogen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkaline (ALK) Electrolyzer</li><li>Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzer</li><li>Solid Oxide Electrolyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Green Hydrogen market is categorized into three main types: Alkaline Electrolyzer, Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzer, and Solid Oxide Electrolyzer. </p><p>The Alkaline Electrolyzer employs a solution of potassium hydroxide or sodium hydroxide as an electrolyte, which facilitates the splitting of water into hydrogen and oxygen. </p><p>The Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzer uses a solid polymer electrolyte to produce hydrogen gas by separating water molecules. This type is known for its compactness, high efficiency, and fast response time. </p><p>The Solid Oxide Electrolyzer operates at high temperatures and utilizes a solid ceramic electrolyte, allowing for the direct conversion of carbon dioxide and water into hydrogen gas. These market types collectively contribute to the growth and advancement of the Green Hydrogen industry.</p></p>
