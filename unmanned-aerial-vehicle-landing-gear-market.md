<p><p>The market for Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) landing gear can be categorized into four types: strut landing gear, rocker landing gear, pontoon landing gear, and framed landing gear. </p><p>Strut landing gear is a rigid structure with supports that act as shock absorbers during landing. </p><p>Rocker landing gear comprises a rocking mechanism that allows the UAV to stabilize on uneven surfaces. </p><p>Pontoon landing gear involves using floatation devices, similar to those on boats, to enable landing on water surfaces. </p><p>Framed landing gear uses a combination of frameworks and supports for stability and impact reduction during landing.</p></p>
