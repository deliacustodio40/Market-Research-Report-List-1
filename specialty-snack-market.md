<p><p>Specialty snacks, also known as premium snacks, are unique and high-quality food products that are targeted towards health-conscious individuals seeking indulgence without compromising on their dietary preferences. With the increasing popularity of vegan and plant-based diets, there is a growing demand for snacks that are free from animal-based ingredients. This trend has led to the emergence of innovative plant-based snacks made from ingredients such as chickpeas, lentils, quinoa, and nuts.</p><p>Another trend in the specialty snack market is the introduction of functional snacks.
