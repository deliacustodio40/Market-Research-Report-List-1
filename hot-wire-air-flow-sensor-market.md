<p><strong>The Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Type</li><li>Mechanical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot wire air flow sensors are used to measure the amount of air flowing into an engine. The electronic type utilizes a heated wire that is cooled by the air passing over it, and the change in electrical resistance is used to calculate the airflow. The mechanical type works on a similar principle, but instead of a heated wire, it uses a vane or flap that moves in response to the airflow and sends a signal to measure it. Both types are commonly used in automotive applications to optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
