It includes two main market types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR meters collect data periodically and require manual intervention for data retrieval, while AMI meters provide continuous data monitoring and communication through a network infrastructure. Both market types aim to improve accuracy, efficiency, and convenience in gas metering, billing, and maintenance processes.</p></p>
