<p><p>The Fire Retarding Hydraulic Fluid market can be divided into four types based on their composition: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-in-oil emulsions and offer good fire resistance but limited lubrication properties. HFB fluids are water-in-oil emulsions with higher water content, providing better fire resistance but less lubrication than HFA fluids. HFC fluids are water glycol-based, offering excellent fire resistance and improved lubrication. HFD fluids, also known as synthetic esters, provide the highest level of fire resistance and superior lubrication properties, making them suitable for high-risk applications.</p></p>
