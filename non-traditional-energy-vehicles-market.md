<p><p>Non-traditional energy vehicles refer to different types of vehicles that are powered by alternative energy sources instead of traditional fossil fuels. Blade Electric Vehicles are fully electric cars that run solely on electricity stored in batteries. Extended Range Electric Vehicles have a larger battery pack and can also use a gasoline engine to generate electricity for longer journeys. Hybrid Electric Vehicles combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, allowing them to switch between the two power sources. Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles utilize hydrogen to power an electric motor, emitting only water as a byproduct. Hydrogen Powered Vehicles are similar but focus solely on using hydrogen as the energy source.</p></p>
