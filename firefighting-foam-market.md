<p><p>Firefighting foam is a crucial tool in controlling and extinguishing fires. There are three main types of firefighting foams: protein foam, synthetic foam, and Class A foam. Protein foam, derived from animal byproducts, is effective on class B fires and creates a thick blanket that suppresses the flames. Synthetic foam, composed of synthetic surfactants, is versatile, capable of controlling class A, B, and C fires, and forms a cohesive film that seals the burning surface. Class A foam is specifically designed for fighting class A fires, improving water penetration and extinguishing capabilities, and reducing re-ignition risks.</p></p>
