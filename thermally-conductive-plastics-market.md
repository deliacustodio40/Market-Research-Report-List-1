<p><p>Thermally Conductive Plastics are a type of plastic materials that have the ability to conduct heat efficiently. There are two main types within this market. Thermally Conductive Insulating Plastic refers to plastics that are good at conducting heat while also providing electrical insulation properties. On the other hand, Thermally and Electrically Conductive Plastics can conduct both heat and electricity effectively. Both types have various applications in industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace, where efficient heat dissipation is critical for component performance and safety.</p></p>
