<p><p>The plastic processing additive market includes various types of additives that enhance the performance and properties of plastics. Plasticizers are used to increase flexibility and reduce brittleness. Heat stabilizers prevent thermal degradation. Antioxidants protect against oxidation and polymers degradation. Light stabilizers prevent color fading and degradation caused by UV light. Flame retardants reduce the flammability of plastics. Lubricants improve the flow of plastic during processing. Other additives include blowing agents, antistatic agents, and impact modifiers, which provide specific functionalities to the plastic material.</p></p>
