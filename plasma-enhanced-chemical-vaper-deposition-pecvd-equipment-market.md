<p><p>The Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) equipment market consists of two main types: plate type and tubular type. Plate type PECVD equipment utilizes a flat plate design, where the substrate is placed on top of the plate and subjected to plasma deposition. On the other hand, tubular type PECVD equipment involves a tubular chamber, where the substrate is inserted through the tube and exposed to plasma for deposition. Both types offer efficient and controlled deposition processes, with plate type suitable for larger substrates and tubular type for smaller ones.</p></p>
