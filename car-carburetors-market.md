<p><strong>The Car Carburetors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float-Feed Carburetor</li><li>Diaphragm Carburetor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car carburetors market consists of two main types: float-feed carburetors and diaphragm carburetors. Float-feed carburetors regulate fuel flow by using a float mechanism that controls the fuel level in the carburetor bowl. This type is commonly found in older vehicles. On the other hand, diaphragm carburetors use a flexible diaphragm to regulate fuel flow, making them more efficient and suitable for modern cars. These carburetors are designed to deliver fuel to the engine based on the air pressure changes in the intake manifold.</p></p>
