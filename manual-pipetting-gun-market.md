<p><p>The manual pipetting gun market offers two types of pipettes: single channel and multichannel. Single channel pipettes are designed for aspirating and dispensing liquids using a single tip at a time. They are widely used in laboratories where precision is crucial. On the other hand, multichannel pipettes are capable of simultaneously aspirating and dispensing multiple samples using several tips. This makes them highly efficient for tasks that require handling multiple samples. Both types of pipettes are commonly used in research, diagnostics, and other laboratory settings.</p></p>
