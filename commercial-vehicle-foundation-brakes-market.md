<p><p>The commercial vehicle foundation brakes market consists of three major types: S-Cam Drum Brakes, Disc Brakes, and Wedge Brakes. S-Cam Drum Brakes are widely used and consists of brake shoes pushed against the drum by a rotating S-cam. Disc Brakes operate by clamping brake pads onto a rotating disc, providing improved braking performance. Wedge Brakes use a wedge-shaped mechanism to generate a force against the brake drum, effectively slowing down the vehicle. These different types offer various braking capabilities and are essential for the safe operation of commercial vehicles.</p></p>
