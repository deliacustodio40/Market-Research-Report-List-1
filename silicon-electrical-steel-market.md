<p><p>Silicon electrical steel is a crucial material used in the manufacturing of various electrical devices. It is available in two market types: oriented type and non-oriented type. Oriented type silicon steel possesses superior magnetic properties due to its specific production process, making it ideal for power transformers and electric motors. Non-oriented type silicon steel, on the other hand, lacks a preferred direction of magnetization and is used for smaller electrical components such as inductors and generators. Both market types cater to different electrical applications, providing efficient and reliable performance in their respective fields.</p></p>
