<p><p>The Reverse Bend Testing Machine market is divided into two types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic machines are designed to perform the entire testing process automatically, from loading the sample to analyzing the results. They require minimal human intervention and are suitable for high-volume testing applications. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention during the testing process, such as sample loading and result interpretation. These machines are more suitable for smaller-scale testing operations where a higher level of human involvement is feasible.</p></p>
