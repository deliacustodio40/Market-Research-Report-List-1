<p><p>Phosphorus ore, also known as phosphate rock, is a sedimentary rock containing high levels of phosphate minerals. It is used as a raw material for the production of various phosphate fertilizers and phosphoric acid. Additionally, the growing adoption of organic farming practices has increased the usage of phosphorus-based organic fertilizers, further contributing to market growth.</p><p>Another key trend in the phosphorus ore market is the expansion of phosphate mining activities in developing regions.
