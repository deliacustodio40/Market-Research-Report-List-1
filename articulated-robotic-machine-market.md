<p><p>The Articulated Robotic Machine market can be classified based on the number of axes they possess. The types include less than 4-axis, 4-axis, 5-axis, 6-axis, and above 6-axis machines. Less than 4-axis machines have limited movement capabilities, while 4-axis machines can move in four directions. 5-axis machines provide more flexibility by allowing rotation on two additional axes. 6-axis machines offer even greater maneuverability with the addition of one more axis. Machines with more than 6-axes have the highest level of articulated movement, allowing for precise and complex tasks to be performed.</p></p>
