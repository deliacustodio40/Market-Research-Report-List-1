<p><p>The computer spring machine market can be categorized into two types: Camless Spring Machine and Cam Spring Machine. </p><p>Camless Spring Machine uses advanced computer technology to control the spring forming process without the need for traditional cam mechanisms. This results in more precise and flexible spring production, allowing for complex designs and quicker setup times.</p><p>On the other hand, Cam Spring Machine relies on cam mechanisms to control the spring forming process. Although it may offer less flexibility and precision compared to the camless counterpart, it is often more cost-effective and suitable for simpler spring production needs.</p></p>
