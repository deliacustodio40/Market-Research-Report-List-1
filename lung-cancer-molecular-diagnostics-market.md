<p><p>Lung cancer molecular diagnostics market includes various types of technologies used for analyzing genetic alterations in lung cancer. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) allows amplification of specific DNA sequences for detection of mutations. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) identifies specific chromosomal abnormalities by fluorescently labeling DNA probes. DNA sequencing determines the order of nucleotides in a DNA molecule, providing detailed genetic information. Gene chip technology helps analyze multiple genes simultaneously. These techniques aid in identifying genetic mutations and alterations in lung cancer patients, assisting in personalized treatment decisions.</p></p>
