<p><p>The brake material market consists of various types of brake components used in automotive and industrial applications. This includes brake blocks which are solid plates usually made of metal or composite materials that create friction against the moving parts to slow down or stop the vehicle. Brake pads are similar but typically made of softer materials, such as organic compounds or ceramics, to provide effective braking without causing excessive wear on the rotors. Brake shoes, on the other hand, are curved metal plates with friction materials attached to contact the brake drums and provide stopping power. Lastly, "others" in the market may include additional brake components like brake discs or rotors.</p></p>
