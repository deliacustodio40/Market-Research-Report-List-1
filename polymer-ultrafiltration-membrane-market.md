<p><p>Polymer Ultrafiltration Membrane market can be classified into two types based on the materials used: inorganic membrane and organic membrane. On the other hand, organic membranes are composed of carbon-based polymers. They provide a higher degree of flexibility and are cost-effective. Both types of membranes have their own advantages and are used in various applications such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
