<p><p>The airbag gas generator market comprises two main types: the double stage gas pyrotechnic type and the double stage gas storage type. In the double stage gas pyrotechnic type, a chemical reaction occurs when the gas generator is triggered, producing gas to inflate the airbag. On the other hand, the double stage gas storage type utilizes stored gas inside the generator, which is released to inflate the airbag when activated. Both types function to rapidly inflate the airbag in the event of a collision, ensuring occupant safety.</p></p>
