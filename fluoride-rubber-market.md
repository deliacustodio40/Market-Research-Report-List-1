<p><p>Fluoride rubber, also known as fluoroelastomers, are a type of synthetic rubber that possess excellent resistance to chemicals, heat, and weathering. There are three main types of fluoride rubber in the market: fluorocarbon elastomers, fluorosilicone elastomers, and perfluoroelastomers. </p><p>Fluorocarbon elastomers have high temperature and fuel resistance, making them suitable for applications in aerospace, automotive, and petroleum industries. </p><p>Fluorosilicone elastomers combine the properties of fluorocarbons with the flexibility of silicone, making them ideal for sealing and gasket applications in extreme environments.</p><p>Perfluoroelastomers are highly resistant to chemicals and have excellent performance at elevated temperatures, making them suitable for use in aggressive chemical processing and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
