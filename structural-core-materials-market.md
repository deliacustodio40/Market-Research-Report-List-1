The market is categorized into different types including Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer (GFRP), Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer (CFRP), Natural Fiber Reinforced Polymer (NFRP), and Others. GFRP utilizes glass fibers embedded in a polymer matrix, while CFRP uses carbon fibers in a polymer matrix. NFRP incorporates natural fibers such as plants or wood in a polymer matrix. The "Others" category consists of alternative core materials like foam, honeycomb, and metal.</p></p>
