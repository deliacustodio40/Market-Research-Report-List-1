<p><p>Specialty fiber optics market includes multimode and single-mode fiber types. Multimode fiber is designed to transmit multiple light signals simultaneously through different modes, making it suitable for short-distance communication within buildings or campuses. On the other hand, single-mode fiber allows only one mode of propagation, enabling the transmission of light signals over long distances without losing its quality. Single-mode fiber is commonly used in long-haul telecommunication networks and data centers. Both these fiber types cater to distinct market needs based on the distance requirements of the communication network.</p></p>
