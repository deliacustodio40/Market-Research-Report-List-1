<p><p>AED Electrode Pads are medical devices that are used in conjunction with Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs) to deliver electric currents to the heart during cardiac arrest in order to restore normal heart rhythms. These electrode pads consist of adhesive pads with conductive gel that are attached to the chest of a patient. They detect the heart's rhythm and send electrical shocks to restore normal heart function. AED electrode pads are an important tool in emergency medical situations, where timely intervention can be critical to saving lives.</p><p>The AED Electrode Pads Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The AED electrode pads market includes two types: pediatric defibrillator pads and adult defibrillator pads. Pediatric defibrillator pads are specifically designed for use on children and infants during cardiac emergencies. These pads deliver lower energy shocks appropriate for their smaller bodies. On the other hand, adult defibrillator pads are designed for use on adults during cardiac emergencies. They deliver higher energy shocks suitable for adult bodies. Both types of electrode pads are essential components of automated external defibrillators (AEDs) used in various settings to administer life-saving electrical shocks to restore normal heart rhythms.</p></p>
