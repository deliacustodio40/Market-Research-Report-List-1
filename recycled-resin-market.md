<p><p>The recycled resin market encompasses various types, including PET, PP, HDPE, and LDPE. PET, or polyethylene terephthalate, is commonly used in packaging materials, particularly beverage bottles. PP, or polypropylene, is utilized in a range of applications such as automotive parts and household goods. HDPE, or high-density polyethylene, finds application in products like pipes, bottles, and containers. Lastly, LDPE, or low-density polyethylene, is commonly used for film packaging and bags. These different types of recycled resins cater to various industries, offering sustainability and cost-effectiveness while reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
