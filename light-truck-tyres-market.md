<p><p>The light truck tires market is divided into two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires are built with a layer of steel belts under the tread which helps to provide a smoother ride, better fuel efficiency, and improved traction. They are known for their high durability and longer lifespan. On the other hand, bias tires are constructed with multiple layers of crisscrossed nylon belts under the tread. They offer excellent load carrying capacity and are more resistant to punctures, making them suitable for off-road or extreme driving conditions.</p></p>
