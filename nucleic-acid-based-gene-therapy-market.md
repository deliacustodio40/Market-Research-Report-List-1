<p><p>Nucleic Acid Based Gene Therapy offers various market types for treating genetic disorders. Anti-Sense and Anti-Gene therapies target specific genes to block their expression. Nucleoside Analogs modify DNA structures to inhibit disease progression. Ribozymes act as enzymes to break down specific RNA molecules. Aptamers are synthetic molecules that bind to specific targets. Other approaches include nucleic acid vaccines and RNA interference. These innovations show promise in revolutionizing the treatment of genetic diseases.</p></p>
