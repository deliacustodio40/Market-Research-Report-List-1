The global maritime industry has been witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing seaborne trade and a rise in maritime tourism. Modern ship horns are equipped with advanced features such as automatic signaling systems, adjustable sound levels, and integration with ship navigation systems. These technological innovations enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of ship horns, making them essential safety equipment for ships.</p><p>Additionally, stringent regulations related to maritime safety standards and guidelines for the use of sound signals are driving market growth. Ship operators are increasingly investing in high-quality ship horns that comply with these regulations, further bolstering the market.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is anticipated to witness growth due to the increasing retrofitting activities in the maritime industry.
