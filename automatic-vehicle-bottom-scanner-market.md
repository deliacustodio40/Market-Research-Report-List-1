<p><p>The automatic vehicle bottom scanner market consists of two main types: fixed and mobile. They continuously scan the undersides of vehicles passing over them to identify any hidden contraband, like drugs or weapons. On the other hand, mobile bottom scanners are portable devices that can be deployed in different locations as needed. They provide flexibility in scanning vehicles at various sites, such as airports, ports, or crime scenes. Both types of scanners aim to enhance security measures and detect potential threats.</p></p>
