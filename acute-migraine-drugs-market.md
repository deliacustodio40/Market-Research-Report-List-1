<p><p>The acute migraine drugs market is categorized into three types: Triptans, NSAIDs, and Others. Triptans are a class of medications that target serotonin receptors to alleviate migraines. They specifically constrict blood vessels and reduce inflammation in the brain. NSAIDs, or nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, work by reducing pain and inflammation associated with migraines. The "Others" category encompasses various drugs such as antiemetics, ergotamines, and opioids that may be prescribed for migraine relief when other options are ineffective or contraindicated. These drugs aim to provide immediate relief and manage the symptoms of an acute migraine attack.</p></p>
