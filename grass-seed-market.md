<p><p>The grass seed market can be categorized into two types: cool season grass and warm season grass. Cool season grasses thrive in cooler climates and are commonly used in regions with moderate temperatures. They include varieties such as Kentucky bluegrass and tall fescue. Warm season grasses, on the other hand, prefer warmer climates and are ideal for areas with hot summers and mild winters. Popular warm season grass varieties include Bermuda grass and St. Augustine grass. These different types cater to various climate conditions and preferences of homeowners and landscapers.</p></p>
