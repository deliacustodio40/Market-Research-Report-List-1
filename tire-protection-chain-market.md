<p><p>The Tire Protection Chain market offers three main types of chains: Link-Ring-System, Ring-Ring-System, and Ring-Locking-System. The Link-Ring-System chains are characterized by links and rings that interlock to protect tires from damage. Ring-Ring-System chains have rings connecting with other rings to form a protective shield. Lastly, Ring-Locking-System chains consist of rings with locking mechanisms to securely fasten the chains around the tires. These different types of chains provide options for customers based on their specific needs and preferences in ensuring tire protection.</p></p>
