<p><p>The solar photovoltaic glass market offers several types of glass that cater to different needs. AR coated glass is designed with an anti-reflective coating, reducing reflection and increasing the amount of sunlight absorbed by solar cells. Tempered glass is strengthened through a heating process, making it more durable and resistant to breakage. TCO (transparent conductive oxide) glass contains thin layers of conductive materials, enabling efficient electricity generation. The market also includes other glass types that are specifically tailored for solar applications, such as textured glass or colored glass, providing flexibility and customization options for solar panel manufacturers.</p></p>
