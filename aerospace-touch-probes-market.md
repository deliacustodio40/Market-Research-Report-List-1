<p><p>The Aerospace Touch Probes market encompasses various types: Contact Type, Electronic Type, Infrared Type, and Scanning Type. Contact Type probes physically touch the surface being measured to gather data. Electronic Type probes use electronic signals to measure the distance between the probe and the surface. Infrared Type probes utilize infrared technology to measure distances, often used in environments that require non-contact measurements. Lastly, Scanning Type probes are capable of scanning large surfaces quickly and efficiently, allowing for detailed measurements. Each type offers unique features and benefits for aerospace applications.</p></p>
