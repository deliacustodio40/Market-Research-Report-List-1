<p><p>The automotive cooling fan market comprises three major types of fans: radiator fans, condenser fans, and heat/ventilation fans. Heat/ventilation fans circulate air inside the vehicle's cabin, ensuring a comfortable temperature for passengers. These fans play crucial roles in maintaining the optimal temperature of various components in an automobile, essential for efficient vehicle performance.</p></p>
