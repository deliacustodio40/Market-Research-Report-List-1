<p><p>Composite wood panels are a type of engineered wood panels made from different wood fibers and adhesives. Hardboard is a composite panel made from compressed wood fiber and resin, commonly used in furniture and construction. Particleboard is composed of wood particles bonded with resin and is mainly used in furniture manufacturing. MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard) is made from fine wood fibers bonded with resin under high pressure and temperature, commonly used in cabinetry and interior design. These types of composite wood panels offer cost-effective and versatile alternatives to solid wood for various applications.</p></p>
