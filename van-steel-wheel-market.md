<p><p>The Van Steel Wheel market primarily consists of three types - casting, forging, and other. Casting involves pouring molten metal into a mold to obtain the desired shape. This method offers cost-effective production and is suitable for small batch sizes. Forging involves heating metal and molding it into shape using extreme pressure. It ensures superior strength and durability. Other market types may include advanced manufacturing techniques like flow forming, where an elongated wheel is created by spinning a metal disc at high speeds, resulting in enhanced strength and reduced weight.</p></p>
