<p><ul><li>Adipates</li><li>Trimellitates</li><li>Benzoates</li><li>Epoxies</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-phthalate based plasticizer market types include adipates, trimellitates, benzoates, epoxies, and others. Adipates are phthalate alternatives commonly used in PVC applications due to their excellent low-temperature properties. Trimellitates are known for their high-temperature performance and are used in wire and cable applications. Benzoates offer good solubility and are used in flooring, medical devices, and food packaging. Epoxies are widely used in adhesives, sealants, and coatings. Lastly, the "others" category includes various types of non-phthalate plasticizers that possess unique properties suited for specific applications.</p></p>
