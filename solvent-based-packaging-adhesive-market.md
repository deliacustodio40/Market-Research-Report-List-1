<p><p>Solvent-based packaging adhesive market consists of two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are premixed and ready to use without any additional mixing or preparation. They are commonly used in packaging applications where fast bonding times are essential. On the other hand, two-component adhesives require the mixing of base and curing agents before application. They are often used in more demanding applications where high strength and durability are required. Both types offer reliable bonding solutions for various packaging materials, providing versatility and efficiency in the market.</p></p>
