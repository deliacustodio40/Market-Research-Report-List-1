<p><p>The Monocrystalline Silicon Ingot market can be categorized into two types: P-Type and N-Type. P-Type ingots are created by introducing elements like boron into the silicon crystal structure, resulting in a positive electrical charge. This type is commonly used in electronic devices, solar cells, and integrated circuits. On the other hand, N-Type ingots are created by introducing elements like phosphorus, which gives a negative electrical charge. These ingots are also used in electronic devices and solar cells, but are known for their higher efficiency and lower sensitivity to impurities.</p></p>
