<p><p>Phenolic resin carbon fiber prepreg is a composite material used in various industries. It is available in two market types: thermoset and thermo-plastic. Thermoset prepreg utilizes a resin that undergoes a chemical reaction upon heating, resulting in a rigid structure with high strength and heat resistance. Thermo-plastic prepreg, on the other hand, uses a resin that remains in a solid state when heated and can be re-molded multiple times. Each type has its unique characteristics, making them suitable for different applications and industries.</p></p>
