<p><p>The industrial wax market is divided into three types: fossil-based, synthetic-based, and bio-based. Fossil-based wax is derived from petroleum or crude oil and is widely used due to its high melting point and hardness. Synthetic-based wax is produced through chemical processes and offers customizable properties such as melting point and hardness. Bio-based wax is made from natural sources such as soybeans or palm oil and is considered a sustainable alternative to fossil-based wax. These wax types cater to different industries and applications based on their unique properties and environmental factors.</p></p>
