<p><p>Pharmaceutical grade cation exchange resins are widely used in the pharmaceutical industry for various applications. They are commonly used for the purification of pharmaceutical products. On the other hand, weakly acidic cation exchange resins have a lower exchange capacity and are suitable for selective removal of specific cations. Both types of resins play an important role in ensuring high-quality pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
