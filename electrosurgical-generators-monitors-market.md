<p><strong>The Electrosurgical Generators Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Generator</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator</li><li>Vessel Sealing Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrosurgical generators monitors are used in surgical procedures to control the electrical energy delivered to tissues. There are three types: Monopole electrosurgical generator, which delivers a single current to cut or coagulate tissue; Bipolar electrosurgical generator, which uses two currents to cut and coagulate tissue simultaneously, reducing the risk of damage to nearby tissue; Vessel sealing generator, which is specifically designed for sealing blood vessels during surgery, minimizing bleeding. These generators are essential tools in various surgical specialties, assisting surgeons in performing precise and safe procedures.</p></p>
