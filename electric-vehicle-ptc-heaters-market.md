<p><p>Electric Vehicle (EV) PTC Heaters are used to provide heating in electric vehicles. There are different types of PTC Heaters available for EVs. Immersion Heaters are installed in the cooling system of the vehicle to heat up the coolant. Radiator Heaters are placed near the vehicle's radiator to warm up the air that enters the cabin. Pad Style Heaters are designed to be directly installed on surfaces like the car seat or the steering wheel to provide localized heating. Each of these heater types serves a specific purpose in maintaining comfortable temperatures inside electric vehicles.</p></p>
