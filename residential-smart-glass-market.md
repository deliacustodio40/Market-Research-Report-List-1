<p><p>The residential smart glass market consists of three main types: electrochromic glass, thermochromic glass, and photochromic glass. Electrochromic glass is capable of changing its tint and transparency with the application of an electrical current. Thermochromic glass adapts its tint based on temperature changes. Lastly, photochromic glass changes its transparency when exposed to UV light. These types of smart glass technologies provide homeowners with the ability to control light, heat, and privacy levels in their homes, making them a popular choice in the residential sector.</p></p>
