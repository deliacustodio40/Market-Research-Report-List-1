<p><p>The achromats and lens systems market encompasses three main types: positive achromats lens, negative achromats lens, and cylindrical achromats lens. Positive achromats lens are designed to correct spherical aberration and minimize chromatic aberration by combining a positive lens element with a negative lens element. Negative achromats lens, on the other hand, correct chromatic aberration by combining a negative lens element with a positive lens element. Cylindrical achromats lens are used specifically to correct astigmatism. These market segments cater to various optical applications, offering solutions to enhance overall image quality.</p></p>
